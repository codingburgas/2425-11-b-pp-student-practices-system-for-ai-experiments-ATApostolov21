System for Experiments with Artificial Intelligence

A web-based system where students and teachers can create, train, and test simple AI models while practicing database work, result visualization, and using basic algorithms.

This platform allows you to:

  • Upload datasets and train various AI models on them
  • Visualize training results and model performance
  • Make predictions with your trained models
  • Share and discuss results with other students and teachers
